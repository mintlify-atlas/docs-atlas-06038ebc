Education entries are used to showcase your academic degrees, certifications, and educational background in your CV.

## Overview

The `EducationEntry` is designed specifically for academic credentials. It prominently displays the institution, degree, and field of study, along with dates, location, and achievements.

## Fields

### Required Fields

<ParamField path="institution" type="string" required>
  The name of the educational institution.
  
  **Examples:**
  - `"Boğaziçi University"`
  - `"MIT"`
  - `"Harvard University"`
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="area" type="string" required>
  The field of study or major.
  
  **Examples:**
  - `"Mechanical Engineering"`
  - `"Computer Science"`
  - `"Electrical Engineering"`
</ParamField>

### Optional Fields

<ParamField path="degree" type="string">
  The degree obtained or pursued.
  
  **Examples:**
  - `"BS"` - Bachelor of Science
  - `"BA"` - Bachelor of Arts
  - `"PhD"` - Doctor of Philosophy
  - `"MS"` - Master of Science
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="start_date" type="string">
  The start date in `YYYY-MM-DD`, `YYYY-MM`, or `YYYY` format.
  
  **Examples:**
  - `"2020-09-24"`
  - `"2020-09"`
  - `"2020"`
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="end_date" type="string">
  The end date in `YYYY-MM-DD`, `YYYY-MM`, or `YYYY` format. Use `"present"` for ongoing education.
  
  **Examples:**
  - `"2024-05-20"`
  - `"2024-05"`
  - `"2024"`
  - `"present"`
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="date" type="string">
  Single date for events without a range. Supports `YYYY-MM-DD`, `YYYY-MM`, `YYYY`, or custom text like `"Fall 2023"`.
  
  Use this instead of `start_date` and `end_date` for single-day events or imprecise dates.
  
  **Examples:**
  - `"2020-09-24"`
  - `"2020"`
  - `"Fall 2023"`
  - `"Summer 2020"`
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="location" type="string">
  The location of the institution.
  
  **Examples:**
  - `"Istanbul, Türkiye"`
  - `"New York, NY"`
  - `"Remote"`
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="summary" type="string">
  A brief description or overview of your education. Supports Markdown.
  
  **Example:**
  ```yaml
  summary: Completed advanced coursework in machine learning and artificial intelligence.
  ```
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="highlights" type="list[string]">
  A list of bullet points highlighting key achievements, coursework, awards, or activities. Supports Markdown.
  
  **Examples:**
  ```yaml
  highlights:
    - "GPA: 3.97/4.00, Valedictorian"
    - "Fulbright Scholarship recipient for Graduate Studies"
    - "Dean's Honor List, Sportsperson of the Year"
  ```
</ParamField>

## Date Behavior

<Note>
  **Important:** Date fields have special behavior:
  - If only `start_date` is provided, `end_date` defaults to `"present"`
  - If only `end_date` is provided, it's treated as a single `date`
  - If `date` is provided, `start_date` and `end_date` are ignored
</Note>

## Examples

### Basic Education Entry

```yaml
education:
  - institution: Boğaziçi University
    area: Mechanical Engineering
    degree: BS
    start_date: 2015-09
    end_date: 2020-06
    location: Istanbul, Turkey
```

### Education with Highlights

```yaml
education:
  - institution: Boğaziçi University
    area: Mechanical Engineering
    degree: BS
    start_date: 2015-09
    end_date: 2020-06
    location: Istanbul, Turkey
    highlights:
      - "GPA: 3.24/4.00 ([Transcript](https://example.com))"
      - "Awards: Dean's Honor List, Sportsperson of the Year"
```

### PhD with Thesis Information

```yaml
education:
  - institution: Princeton University
    area: Computer Science
    degree: PhD
    start_date: 2018-09
    end_date: 2023-05
    location: Princeton, NJ
    highlights:
      - 'Thesis: Efficient Neural Architecture Search for Resource-Constrained Deployment'
      - 'Advisor: Prof. Sanjeev Arora'
      - NSF Graduate Research Fellowship, Siebel Scholar (Class of 2022)
```

### Bachelor's Degree with Honors

```yaml
education:
  - institution: Boğaziçi University
    area: Computer Engineering
    degree: BS
    start_date: 2014-09
    end_date: 2018-06
    location: Istanbul, Türkiye
    highlights:
      - 'GPA: 3.97/4.00, Valedictorian'
      - Fulbright Scholarship recipient for Graduate Studies
```

### Ongoing Education

```yaml
education:
  - institution: Stanford University
    area: Artificial Intelligence
    degree: MS
    start_date: 2023-09
    end_date: present
    location: Stanford, CA
```

## Markdown Support

All text fields support Markdown formatting:

```yaml
education:
  - institution: MIT
    area: Computer Science
    degree: BS
    start_date: 2018-09
    end_date: 2022-05
    location: Cambridge, MA
    highlights:
      - "GPA: **3.95**/4.00"
      - "Relevant coursework: *Machine Learning*, *Computer Vision*, *NLP*"
      - "[Research publication](https://example.com) on neural networks"
```

## Tips

<Tip>
  **Use highlights effectively:**
  - Start with your GPA if it's strong (3.5+)
  - Include relevant coursework for early-career CVs
  - Mention scholarships, fellowships, and awards
  - Add thesis title and advisor for graduate degrees
  - Use Markdown links for transcripts or thesis papers
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Date formatting:**
  - Use `YYYY-MM` format (e.g., `2020-09`) for academic dates
  - Always use `"present"` for ongoing education, not "current" or other terms
  - RenderCV will automatically calculate and display the duration
</Tip>
